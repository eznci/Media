What Is SOCIAL MEDIA: WHAT IS IT AND WHY IS IT USED?

Social media refers to online platforms and websites that allow users to create and share content, as well as engage in social networking. These platforms are designed to facilitate communication, collaboration, and the sharing of information, ideas, and multimedia content in a virtual environment.

Definition and Types of Social Media

Social media refers to online platforms and technologies that enable users to create, share, and exchange information, ideas, and multimedia content. Common types of social media include social networking sites (e.g., Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn), content-sharing platforms (e.g., Instagram, YouTube), messaging apps (e.g., WhatsApp, Messenger), and microblogging sites (e.g., Twitter).
